# Product Overview

ShipAny Template Two is an AI SaaS boilerplate/starter kit for rapidly building and launching AI-powered SaaS applications.

## Core Value Proposition
- Pre-built infrastructure for AI SaaS products
- Multi-tenant ready with authentication, payments, and credits system
- Internationalization (i18n) support out of the box
- Themeable landing pages with configurable sections

## Key Features
- User authentication with better-auth (social login, email verification)
- Payment processing (Stripe, PayPal, Creem)
- Credits/subscription management system
- AI integrations (Replicate, Gemini, OpenRouter)
- Admin dashboard for user/content management
- Blog, docs, and changelog support via MDX
- Role-based access control (RBAC)

## Target Users
Developers building AI-powered SaaS products who want to skip boilerplate setup and focus on their core AI features.
