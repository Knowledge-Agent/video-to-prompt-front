## Product positioning and workflow update

Video to Prompt v2.0 focused on clarifying the product narrative across the public site.

### Updated in v2.0

- Homepage repositioned around prompt-package generation
- Blog content aligned with shot language, workflow design, and prompt QA
- AI tool page reframed around reference-video analysis
- Legal and docs content rewritten for the product instead of template copy
- Changelog and public messaging aligned to Video to Prompt branding

### Why this mattered

A strong workflow product needs a clear public explanation. v2.0 improved how we communicate the value of video-to-prompt as a real production system, not just a feature demo.
