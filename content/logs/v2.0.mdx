Version 2.0 is a major product-quality release.

We redesigned the product around one clear user job:

**Upload one video, decompose prompts, copy and imitate.**

## Improvements in v2.0

- Reworked generator panel for lower cognitive load
- Removed redundant fields and non-core interactions
- Optimized output sections for direct copy-and-use
- Updated landing content to align with real usage scenarios
- Refined multilingual copy for Chinese and English pages

## SEO and content updates

- Reframed metadata around video-to-prompt intent
- Added more relevant blog articles for prompt workflow search demand
- Improved page-level positioning for product fit clarity

## Quality focus

This version targets execution reliability over feature sprawl.

The goal is to help creators and teams move from reference clip to production prompt package faster, with fewer decisions and less confusion.
