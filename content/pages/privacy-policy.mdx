## Introduction

Video to Prompt respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it when you use our service.

We may collect:

- **Account information** such as your email address and basic profile data
- **Usage data** such as product interactions, feature usage, and error logs
- **Uploaded content metadata** needed to process your request and improve service reliability
- **Billing information** when you purchase paid plans or credits
- **Device and browser data** for performance, security, and compatibility

## Why We Collect It

We use this information to:

- provide and maintain the service
- process prompt-package generation requests
- improve workflow quality and product reliability

- prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with trusted service providers that help us operate infrastructure, analytics, authentication, storage, or payments.

## Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. No online system is perfectly secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.
