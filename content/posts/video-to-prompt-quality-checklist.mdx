# Video-to-Prompt Quality Checklist for Teams

When teams scale prompt generation, quality drift is inevitable without a review checklist.

Use this quick QA before accepting a prompt package.

## 1) Structural completeness

- Summary is present and specific
- Master prompt is detailed and coherent
- Short prompt is concise and usable
- Negative prompt includes realistic failure guards
- Shot list has sequence logic
- Keywords are searchable and non-redundant

## 2) Creative consistency

- Tone matches the target project
- Style tags do not conflict
- Camera language is explicit
- Lighting and mood are aligned

## 3) Execution readiness

- Works within target tool token limits
- Easy to copy into your generation stack
- Contains enough constraints to reduce variance

## A simple scoring model

Rate each package on three axes (1-5):

- Clarity
- Control
- Reusability

Only ship packages scoring 4+ on all three.

## Final takeaway

High-performing teams don't rely on inspiration alone. They standardize prompt quality with repeatable QA gates. A checklist turns prompt generation into an operational advantage.
