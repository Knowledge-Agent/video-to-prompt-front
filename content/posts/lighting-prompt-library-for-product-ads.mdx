# Lighting Prompt Library for Product Ads

In product videos, lighting drives perceived quality more than almost any other element. If you want reliable **video generation prompts**, build a dedicated lighting phrase library.

![In product videos, lighting drives perceived quality more than almost any other ](/imgs/blog/tavily/lighting-prompt-library-for-product-ads-2.png)

## Lighting blocks by goal

### Premium studio look

- soft key light from 45-degree front-left
- clean rim light to separate edges
- subtle blue fill on shadow side

### Warm lifestyle look

- window-like side light with soft falloff
- warm bounce on skin and product surface
- low-contrast ambient background

### Dramatic cinematic look

- narrow top light with controlled spill
- deep shadow pockets for contrast
- colored practical light in background

## Example ad prompt line

```text
Matte skincare bottle on stone pedestal, slow dolly-in close-up, 50mm shallow depth, soft key from front-left with clean rim separation, warm bounce fill, premium minimal mood, ending on logo lockup.
```

## QA checklist for lighting prompts

- is key direction clear?
- are shadow areas intentional?
- does lighting match brand mood?
- is reflection control specified?

## FAQ

### Should I add color temperature values?

Only when consistency is critical across many assets.

![Only when consistency is critical across many assets.](/imgs/blog/tavily/lighting-prompt-library-for-product-ads-3.webp)

### Why do highlights look noisy sometimes?

The prompt lacks reflection constraints and surface material clarity.
